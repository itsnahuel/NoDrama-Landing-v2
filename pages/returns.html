Returns

Return Policy

We want you to love your NoDrama Records merchandise. If you're not completely satisfied, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery.

Return Requirements

  • Items must be in original condition with all tags attached
  • Items must be unwashed and unworn
  • Items must be returned in original packaging when possible
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer
  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return

How to Return

Step 1: Contact our customer service team at support@nodramarecords.com with your order number and reason for return.

Step 2: We'll provide you with a return authorization number and return address.

Step 3: Package your items securely and include the return authorization number.

Step 4: Ship the package back to us using a trackable shipping method.

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size or item, please return your original order and place a new order for the desired item.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items damaged by normal wear and tear
  • Items returned after 30 days
  • Items that have been washed or altered
  • Sale or clearance items (marked as final sale)
