Size Guide

T-Shirt Sizing

All measurements are in inches. Our shirts feature an oversized streetwear fit.

Size Chest Width Body Length Sleeve Length
S 20" 27" 8"
M 22" 28" 8.5"
L 24" 29" 9"
XL 26" 30" 9.5"

How to Measure

  • Chest Width: Measure across the chest from armpit to armpit
  • Body Length: Measure from the top of the shoulder to the bottom hem
  • Sleeve Length: Measure from the shoulder seam to the end of the sleeve

Fit Notes

  • Our shirts are designed with an oversized, relaxed fit
  • If you prefer a more fitted look, consider sizing down
  • All shirts are 100% premium cotton and may shrink slightly after washing
  • For the best fit, follow care instructions and wash in cold water
